Get Started







Determine Objectives

Determine what ways you are trying to engage your students. Observe them in the hallways, conduct surveys. Don't focus on what the students aren't doing but what you could be doing better.

Create Content

Fill your school page with content tailored to your student. Give them important information, let them know about upcoming events, and populate your home page with content about whats going on in your school.

Track Your Success

Once you've created your school page, it's important to track your success with the service. We have set up a data analytics that will give your administrators telling information about your student body.
